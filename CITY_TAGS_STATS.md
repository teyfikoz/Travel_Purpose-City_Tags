## üìä Overview

The City Tags database provides simplified travel purpose classification for 800 major cities worldwide using 7 core tags:

- **BUSINESS**: Business hubs, financial centers, MICE destinations
- **LEISURE**: Tourist destinations, cultural sites, beach resorts
- **SEAMAN**: Major ports and crew change facilities
- **CRUISE**: Popular cruise destinations and ports of call
- **MEDICAL**: Medical tourism hubs
- **RELIGIOUS**: Pilgrimage sites and religious destinations
- **SECONDHOME**: Popular second home destinations (future use)

Each city can have up to **3 tags** for multi-purpose classification.
